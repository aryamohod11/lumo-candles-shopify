Our Story

Crafted for
your sanctuary

Lumo began as a small kitchen experiment — a desire to make homes feel like they truly belong to the people living in them. Every candle is hand-poured in small batches using 100% natural soy and coconut wax, free of toxins and synthetic dyes.

We believe scent is memory. Each fragrance is thoughtfully composed to anchor a feeling — calm, warmth, presence.
